We are Stat Track

We are three International Burch University students born and living in a small European country - Bosnia and Herzegovina. We are owners and creators of Stat Track platform. We are passionate gamers and programmers so we decided to make Stat Track, a platform to track your League of Legends statistics. We are looking to make more similar platforms to Stat Track in the future, or even expand Stat Track to more games.
